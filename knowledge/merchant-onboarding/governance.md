# Governance Structure — Merchant Onboarding

> **Template file.** Copy to `~/.project-hub/knowledge/merchant-onboarding/governance.md`
> and replace with your organization's actual governance content.

## Purpose

Defines roles, responsibilities, and decision-making authority across teams involved
in the merchant onboarding process.

## Governance Principles

- Clear ownership per phase
- Escalation paths documented
- Regular touchpoints defined
- Deviation approval process established

## Key Functions & Responsibilities

### Onboarding Project Management

**Owns:** End-to-end coordination, merchant communication, timeline, risk tracking

Key responsibilities:
- Merchant kick-off and onboarding plan
- Stakeholder alignment across internal teams
- Go/No-Go decisions per phase gate
- Escalation to senior management when required

### Commercial / Account Management

**Owns:** Commercial relationship, deal terms, pricing alignment

Key responsibilities:
- Merchant Deal Sheet ownership
- Deviation identification and escalation
- Commercial sign-off at approval phase

### Legal / Compliance

**Owns:** Contract review, regulatory requirements, data processing agreements

Key responsibilities:
- Contract template selection
- Deviation review and approval
- Compliance sign-off

### Technology / Integration

**Owns:** Technical integration, API connectivity, testing environment

Key responsibilities:
- Technical specification and sandbox access
- Integration support and review
- UAT coordination and sign-off

### Risk & Fraud

**Owns:** Risk assessment, fraud rules, monitoring setup

Key responsibilities:
- Merchant risk scoring
- Deviation approval for risk-related items
- Go-Live risk clearance

### Finance / Treasury

**Owns:** Payment flows, settlement, invoicing setup

Key responsibilities:
- Settlement account setup
- Invoice configuration
- First-settlement validation

## Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Owner | Resolution Target |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| Standard deviation | Commercial Lead | 3 business days |
| High-impact deviation | Head of Onboarding | 5 business days |
| Strategic exception | VP / C-Level | Case-by-case |

## Phase Gate Approvals

Each phase requires sign-off before proceeding:

| Phase | Required Approvers |
|-------|--------------------|
| Deviation Assessment | Commercial, Risk, Legal |
| Formal Approval | All functions above |
| Contracting | Legal, Commercial |
| Go-Live | Tech, Risk, Finance, Onboarding PM |
