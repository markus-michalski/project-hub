# Standard Roles — Merchant Onboarding

> **Template file.** Copy to `~/.project-hub/knowledge/merchant-onboarding/roles.md`
> and replace with your organization's actual roles, team names, and contacts.

## Internal Roles

| Role | Team | Responsibilities |
|------|------|-----------------|
| Onboarding Project Manager | Onboarding | End-to-end coordination, merchant communication |
| Account Manager | Commercial | Commercial relationship, escalation point |
| Integration Engineer | Technology | API integration, technical support |
| Risk Analyst | Risk & Fraud | Merchant risk scoring, fraud rule setup |
| Legal Counsel | Legal | Contract review, compliance sign-off |
| Compliance Officer | Compliance | Regulatory requirements per market |
| Finance Controller | Finance | Settlement setup, invoice configuration |
| Product Manager | Product | Product scope, feature requirements |

## External Roles (Merchant Side)

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|-----------------|
| Merchant Project Manager | Coordinates merchant-side implementation |
| Technical Lead | API integration, development |
| Business Owner | Commercial decisions, contract approval |
| Finance Contact | Settlement and invoicing setup |
| Legal Contact | Contract negotiation |

## RACI Matrix (Simplified)

| Activity | Onboarding PM | Commercial | Tech | Risk | Legal | Finance |

| Deviation Assessment | R | C | C | C | C | I |
| Approval Submission | A | R | I | R | R | I |
| Contract Prep | I | C | I | I | R | I |
| Integration | C | I | R | I | I | I |
| UAT | A | I | C | I | I | I |
| Go-Live Sign-off | A | I | R | R | I | R |
| Hypercare | R | C | C | C | I | I |
