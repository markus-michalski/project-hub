## Change Request Process

All scope changes must follow this process before any additional work begins.

**Steps:**

1. Either party identifies a scope change.
2. Requesting party completes the CR Form below and submits to both engagement leads.
3. Consultant assesses impact on timeline, budget, and resources within [3] business days.
4. Both parties sign the CR before work starts.
5. CR is appended to this document and referenced in meeting notes.
