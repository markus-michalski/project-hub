## Purpose

Defines the roles on both the consultant side and the client side, their responsibilities,
and the RACI for key consulting activities. Agree this at kick-off and update when people change.

---

## Consultant-Side Roles

### Lead Consultant

**Owns:** Overall delivery quality, client relationship, escalation management

Key responsibilities:
- Single point of accountability for engagement outcomes
- Chairs steering committee and key workshops
- Reviews and signs off all deliverables before client submission
- Manages scope and raises Change Requests when needed
- Escalates blockers to client sponsor when unresolved after [X] days

### Subject Matter Expert (SME)

**Owns:** Content quality, analysis, research, specialist input

Key responsibilities:
- Conducts interviews, workshops, and data analysis
- Authors core content of deliverables
- Reviews client-provided data and flags gaps
- Supports Lead Consultant in scope assessment for CRs

### Project Coordinator

**Owns:** Logistics, scheduling, document management, status tracking

Key responsibilities:
- Maintains project plan and milestone tracker
- Schedules meetings and sends agendas
- Manages document versioning and shared folder structure
- Tracks open actions and follows up with owners

## Client-Side Roles

### Executive Sponsor

**Owns:** Strategic alignment, final approval, budget release

Key responsibilities:
- Chairs or approves steering committee decisions
- Resolves escalations that cannot be handled at working level
- Signs off final deliverables and authorises payments
- Provides organisational backing for project access and inputs

### Project Owner

**Owns:** Day-to-day coordination on client side, input provision

Key responsibilities:
- Primary counterpart to Lead Consultant
- Organises internal stakeholder access (interviews, data)
- Reviews draft deliverables and consolidates feedback

## RACI — Consulting Activities

RACI: **R** = Responsible, **A** = Accountable, **C** = Consulted, **I** = Informed

| Activity | Lead Consultant | SME | Project Coordinator | Project Owner (Client) | Exec Sponsor |
|----------|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Kick-off meeting | A | R | R | C | I |
| Project plan & schedule | A | C | R | C | I |
| Workshop facilitation | A | R | C | C | I |
| Data / input collection | C | R | I | A | I |
| Deliverable drafting | A | R | I | I | I |
| Deliverable review (internal) | A | C | I | I | I |
| Deliverable review (client) | C | C | I | A | I |
| Deliverable sign-off | C | I | I | R | A |
| Change Request initiation | A | C | R | C | I |
| Change Request approval | C | I | I | C | A |
| Escalation (working level) | A | I | I | A | I |
| Escalation (executive) | R | I | I | C | A |
| Steering committee | R | C | I | C | A |
| Invoice submission | A | I | R | I | I |
| Invoice approval (client) | I | I | I | R | A |
| Project closure / lessons learned | A | C | R | C | I |
